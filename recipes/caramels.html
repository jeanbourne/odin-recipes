Caramels

Delicious Homemade Caramels

This fool-proof caramel recipe will have all your friends asking for more

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Cream
  • 1 stick Butter
  • 1/4 Cup Karo Syrup
  • 1 Cup Sugar

Instruction

  1. Mix ingredients and cook over med low heat until butter is melted.
  2. Turn heat to low and cook until reaches 240 temperature.
  3. Remove from heat, pour into a parchment lined pan, and cool
